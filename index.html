简介 | About

王建勇于2018年在四川大学获得计算机科学与技术博士学位，导师是 章毅教授 。现于 四川大学机器智能实验室 ，从事博士后研究工作， 研究方向为神经网络和大数据。

Jianyong, Wang recieved Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Sichuan University, Chengdu, China, in 2018, surprivised by Prof. Zhang Yi. He is currenctly a postdoctoral fellow with Machine Intelligence Laboratory , Sichuan University. His research intrest includes neural networks and big data.
