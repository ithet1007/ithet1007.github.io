简介 | About

何涛于2017年在四川大学计算机科学与技术攻读博士学位，导师是 章毅教授，IEEE Fellow 。于 四川大学机器智能实验室 ，从事人工智能、医学图像、口腔智能医学等研究。

Tao He is a Ph.D. candidate in computer science and technology from Sichuan University, Chengdu, China, from 2017, surprivised by Prof. Zhang Yi, IEEE Fellow. His research intrest includes Deep Learning, Medical Imaging, and Stomatology Intelligent Medical in Machine Intelligence Laboratory.
